Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new stock to my portfolio?

Go to the Portfolio page and click the "Add Stock" button. Enter the stock symbol and quantity, then submit.

How often is the news updated?

News updates every 30 minutes to keep you informed with the latest market developments.

Can I export my portfolio data?

Yes, on the Portfolio page, click the "Export" button to download your data as a CSV file.
